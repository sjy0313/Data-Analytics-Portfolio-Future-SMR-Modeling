### 3. üìâ Local Extinction Crisis Analysis & Prediction
**"Predicting regional extinction risks using Machine Learning Ensemble Models"**

* **Summary:** Analyzed demographic data to identify regions at risk of local extinction and developed a classification model to predict risk levels.
* **Applied Mathematics & Statistics Connection:**
    * **Multicollinearity Analysis (VIF):** Applied Variance Inflation Factor (VIF) to detect and remove highly correlated independent variables, ensuring the stability of the regression coefficients.
    * **Ensemble Learning:** Utilized **Gradient Boosting (GBM)** and **Random Forest** algorithms, leveraging the law of large numbers to reduce variance and overfitting.
    * **Hyperparameter Optimization:** Used **RandomizedSearchCV** to find the optimal global solution in the parameter space.
* **Key Results:**
    * Achieved high classification accuracy (F1-Score optimized) for identifying high-risk regions.
    * Identified key factors contributing to population decline (e.g., lack of medical/educational infrastructure).
* **Tech:** `Python Scikit-learn', `VIF Analysis`, 'Cross Validation'
